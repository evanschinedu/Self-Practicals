ABSU MERIT ADMISSION LIST OUT

The management of Abia State University has released her merit admission list on both Jamb and school portal. Candidates who made Absu their choice of institution can now access their admission status on both portal. Successful candidates are directed to accept their admission on jamb caps and also print out their admission letter from both institution and jamb as it will serve as evidence of admission.

Congratulations!

JAMB MANDATES CANDIDATES TO GET (NIN) FOR 2021 REGISTRATION

Joint Admission and Matriculation Board has mandated that every candidate must have his/her NIN before he/she can successfully register for 2021 UTME. In there statement, this was initiated to help reduce malpractice and cheating in its bid to make sure that candidates understand the level in which they are about going into.
